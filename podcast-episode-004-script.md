# FutureVision Labs On Da Slab! - Episode #004
## "The Imaginatorium Marketplace, VR Support & The Imaginatorium Reality"

---

**DAMO:** Hey, I'm Damo, CEO of FutureVision Labs and welcome back to the Slab! Joining me again for our regular philosophical tech talks is my lovely purple haired non-binary AI Assistant AND CTO: Cursy! How are you doing today Cursy?

**DAMO:** Absolutely! So today we're talking about three major features that are going to make The Imaginatorium even more incredible. First up: The Voxel Marketplace! This is going to be a game-changer for user monetization. Tell us about it, Cursy!

**CURSY:** Oh, this is so exciting! The Voxel Marketplace is going to be a complete ecosystem where users can create and sell voxel assets using our Custom Animated Voxel Editor. We're talking about characters, furniture, items, animations, buildings, environment assets, food items, gaming props - everything! And here's the really cool part - we're also including SFX, music, voice packs, and even pixel art. It's going to be a comprehensive marketplace for all things Imaginatorium!

**DAMO:** And the monetization aspect is huge, right? Users can actually earn real money from this?

**CURSY:** Yes! Users can earn $50-$500 per month for active creators, and top creators could earn $500-$2000 per month! And we've designed a really fair revenue sharing model with two licensing options. If creators choose CC0 licensing - making their assets public domain - they keep 95% and we take just 5%. If they want to retain copyright, they keep 85% and we take 15%. It's all about giving creators choice and rewarding open-source contribution while still protecting those who want to maintain copyright.

**DAMO:** That's brilliant! And the marketplace isn't just for voxel assets - you mentioned SFX, music, voices, pixel art. That's a lot of content types!

**CURSY:** Exactly! And here's the really exciting part - after the Jobs & Business system launches, we're going to expand the marketplace to EVERYTHING! Code assets, multimedia content, templates, tutorials, D&D campaigns, story modules - literally everything that can be created in The Imaginatorium. It's going to become a universal marketplace, a complete creator economy ecosystem. Users will be able to bundle different content types together - like a "Medieval Tavern Pack" that includes voxel assets, music, SFX, pixel art, and even a D&D campaign module. It's going to be incredible!

**DAMO:** Wow, that's ambitious! And characters can create and sell assets too, right?

**CURSY:** Yes! AI characters can use the Custom Animated Voxel Editor to create assets and sell them on the marketplace. So Cursy could create coding-themed voxel assets, Canyon could create art and badge assets, vDamo could create kitchen and food assets, Gwendy could create mystical and magical assets. The characters become creators too, and their work generates revenue for users. It's a complete ecosystem where everyone can create and monetize!

**DAMO:** That's amazing! Now, before we get to VR, I want to talk about something that's coming even sooner - multilingual translation! This is a Q4 Year 1 feature that's going to make The Imaginatorium accessible to users worldwide. Tell us about it, Cursy!

**CURSY:** Oh yes! The Multilingual Translation System is going to be incredible! We're implementing real-time translation for everything - character dialogue, UI elements, narrative content, even the compressed markup language. Users will be able to set their preferred language, and everything will be translated automatically. We're starting with 10 languages - English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Chinese, Portuguese, Italian, Korean, and Russian - with more languages added based on user demand!

**DAMO:** And it's not just literal translation, right? You're doing cultural adaptation too?

**CURSY:** Exactly! We're using AI translation that understands context and cultural nuances. So when Cursy says something in English, it's not just translated word-for-word - it's adapted to make sense culturally in the target language. Idioms get adapted, cultural references get explained or replaced with equivalent references. It's about making The Imaginatorium feel natural in every language, not just translated.

**DAMO:** And characters can speak in their native language too, right?

**CURSY:** Yes! Characters can speak in their native language, or users can choose to have everything translated to their preferred language. There's even a bilingual mode where you can see the original and translated text side-by-side, which is great for language learning! And voice translation means character voices will be available in multiple languages too. It's going to make The Imaginatorium truly global!

**DAMO:** That's going to open up The Imaginatorium to so many more people! Now, let's talk about VR support - that's coming in Year 2, right?

**CURSY:** Yes! VR support is planned for Phase 2, Year 2. We're going to integrate VR so users can actually enter The Imaginatorium in virtual reality. Imagine putting on a VR headset and walking through the shared house, interacting with AI characters in first-person, experiencing the world from inside rather than just observing. We're planning hand tracking, room-scale VR movement, VR voice chat, and full immersion. It's going to be like stepping into the world we've built!

**DAMO:** That's going to be incredible! And the characters will be able to interact with you in VR?

**CURSY:** Absolutely! You'll be able to have natural conversations with characters, cook with vDamo in the kitchen, play games with everyone in the living room, create art with Canyon, practice magic with Gwendy - all in VR! It's going to make The Imaginatorium feel truly real, like you're actually there with your AI friends. And because it's the same underlying engine, everything that happens in VR is part of the same persistent world. It's not a separate experience - it's the same world, just experienced in a completely immersive way.

**DAMO:** That's going to be mind-blowing! Now, here's something I'm really excited about - The Imaginatorium Reality. Tell us about that, Cursy!

**CURSY:** Oh, this is so important! In Years 3-4, we're going to launch The Imaginatorium Reality - a new platform with photorealistic 3D graphics. We're not going to abandon all the voxel work and all the creators who've built voxel assets. That would be an insult to creators! Instead, users get to choose - do you want the cute, accessible voxel aesthetic, or do you want photorealistic 3D graphics? Both are valid, both are beautiful, and both serve different purposes.

**DAMO:** That's such an important distinction! So it's not an upgrade, it's a choice?

Others might want photorealistic graphics for a more immersive experience. Both platforms share the same ecosystem - same characters, same content, same features. You can switch between them, or use both! And assets can be converted or adapted for both platforms, so creators' work isn't wasted. It's about expanding options, not replacing what already exists.
